Market Safe!

An all-in-one shop visit planning and contact tracing app!

As our society emerges from the COVID - 19 pandemic, we need a way to be socially distant while still getting the resources we need from stores outside. Market Safe is a tool that can help us prevent a second wave of the virus. It allows businesses and customers to connect, plan out the visitors coming into the store, and also functions as a contact tracing site to alert businesses and customers that may have been in contact with a user who tested positive.
